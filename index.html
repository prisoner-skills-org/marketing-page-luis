This app allows prison management to broadcast the skill of inmates to make better use of their time using their skills to make a wage and becoming more employable after prison.

This app contains two user types. You can use it as a prison admin or as a single user who can view posted profiles by prison. Prison admins are able to log in and create profiles for their inmates. The ability to view profiles created by prison administrators is aimed at single users who are potential employers.

For potential employer - No log in needed. You get access to a list of prisons who have posted prisoner profiles. In this list you can see how many profiles each prison has listed and where the prison is located. Clicking on a prison takes you to the profile pages of the prisoners.

For prison admins - After an admin logs in, they are directed to a page where they can see the people’s profiles they’ve created in a list view. In this list they have the possibility to edit the created profiles and they have the option to create new ones.

Advantages for prisons

  • Better use of prisoners
  • More efficient allocation of prisoner jobs
  • Commission for mediated prisoners

Advantages for inmates

  • Can make better use of detention time
  • Better chances after release from prison
  • Better opportunity to earn money in prison

Advantages for employers

  • Favourable labour force
  • Prison-confirmed skills
  • A flagship for the company's social commitment

Advantages for government

  • Fewer recidivist criminals through new jobs
  • New source for staff in public jobs
  • Higher tax revenues through more employment
